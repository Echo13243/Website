Buddhism

Buddha was deemed to have passed outside of history at his death, and so his presence was symbolized by things like the Bodhi Tree, the tree which Buddha sat under when he became enlightened.

The Bodhi Tree today is inside Bodh Gaya, one of the Buddhist pilgrimage sites.

Buddha is also depicted in many different ways, each feature is for a reason, like his half-closed eyes which suggest depth of meditation and detachment. He can be standing, seated, or reclining chiseled into rock, molded in clay, cut in stone, or cast in bronze.
