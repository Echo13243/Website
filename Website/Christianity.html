Christianity

The cross is often associated with the event of Jesus Christ‘s crucifixion and death, but can interpreted in multiple ways.

The mystery of the cross can be used to explain part of the cosmos

The cross can also be seen as linked to the mystery of creation as well as redemption.

The cross was also a very important part of, what was then, modern technology. "Could one cut through the sea if that trophy was not raised intact on the ship in the shape of a sail? Is work possible without the cross? Can pioneers or manual laborers work without instruments bearing that shape?"

The cross meant Christ‘s power and the things he accomplished in his lifetime, and the cross is a very powerful symbol and shape.
