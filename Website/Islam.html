Islam

The most well-known symbol of Islam is the Star and Crescent.

The symbol was not originally Islamic, but of the Ottoman Empire which slowly became associated with the Islam faith.

Another way to represent Islam is the Arabic character for Allah, or God.

The Islamic faith believes in the same God as Judaism and Christianity, or "People of the Book"

The People of the Book share the same monotheistic faith, but Islam is set apart because their prophet is believed to be the last, and their god goes by a different name, which is why the Arabic character for Allah separates Islam from the rest of the People of the Book.
